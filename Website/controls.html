Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move the player character
    • Walk into an enemy to attack them
  • E: Pickup items
  • Q or J: Drop item
  • TAB: Open inventory
    • Use letter keys in inventory to choose an item
  • I or B: Open character screen
  • CAPS LOCK: Free camera
  • >: Go down to the next floor
  • ENTER: Choose an enemy to cast a spell on
