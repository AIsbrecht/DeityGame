How to play

  1. Click here to access the github repository
  2. Click 'Code', the green button just right of the center, and press 'Download ZIP'
  3. Go to your 'Downloads' folder and unzio the folder by right clicking and selecting 'unzip'
  4. Click here to go to the Visual Studio Code website. Download the version for your operating system (Windows, Mac, Linux, etc.) and install VSCode.
  5. Launch VSCode and press 'File', 'Open Folder', and select the folder which you just unzipped.
  6. Open the 'Game_Testing' folder on the left panel and navigate to main.py()
